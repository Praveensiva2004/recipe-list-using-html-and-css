Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

this apple pie recipe has been in family for generations and never fails to delight

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tps ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium sized apples,peeled and sliced

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375'f
  2. In a bowl mix the sugar and cinnamon
  3. toss the apple in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
